03 · The Case for Pausing

Why pause
now

Tactical Churn
Three Migrations in Three Years
Thrive in 2025, Eze upgrade on Thrive in 2026, then migrate that same Eze off Thrive. Each one costs staff time, UAT, cutover risk. The upgrade is building on a foundation we're about to replace.
Better Path
Upgrade Should Happen as Part of PaaS
SS&C stands up a fresh Eze instance for PaaS — deploying the current version, not our four-year-old one. The upgrade is included. UAT can begin in weeks.
Blocked
Thrive Can't Deliver a Working UAT
5+ weeks blocked. Citrix sessions disconnect, servers had 2 CPUs hitting 100%, SentinelOne/FSLogix misconfigured. Multiple "resolutions" haven't fixed it.
Untested
Current DR Has Never Been Tested
Thrive's RTO/RPO is a contractual SLA we've never exercised. SS&C PaaS includes Enhanced DR — tested, maintained, and owned by the vendor who built the application.
Simplification
FIX Connectivity Moves Too
Bloomberg ZFP and broker FIX sessions migrate into SS&C's cloud. Thrive residual shrinks to AD and colo only. Cost escalation ($292K→$441K) becomes avoidable.
Capacity
Capacity Is the Constraint
A three-person team carries AI governance, BeyondTrust remediation, and Thrive contract planning. Q2 on a throwaway upgrade is not the right use of limited resources.

05 · Risks & Dependencies

What could
go wrong

Dependency
SS&C Must Confirm Upgrade Is Included
If required as prerequisite, pause becomes delay. Mitigation: confirm with Jacob Cooper before ORMC.
Version Risk
Running 4-Year-Old Eze Version During Transition
Confirm current version remains supported. No known compliance exposure, but should be documented.
Sunk Cost
Thrive UAT Work Is Sunk Cost
Test plans and validation scripts transfer. Infra-specific work (Citrix config, server setup) does not.
Go-Live Gate
FIX Connectivity Migration
Bloomberg ZFP and broker FIX sessions must be validated in SS&C's cloud before cutover. Plan for parallel testing.
Transition
DR Transition Timing
Current Thrive DRaaS remains in place until SS&C Enhanced DR is validated. No gap in coverage during transition.
